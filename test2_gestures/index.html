Thank you for participating in our Human Computer Interaction research project! This experiment is divided into 2 sections: Vibrations and Gestures

Instructions for Part 2: Gestures

  • In this first section, you will learn 2 different sets of gestures and respond to incoming emoticon vibrations
  • In both gesture sets, you will respond to Vibration Set 1 from the previous part of the experiment
  • There are 6 gestures in each vibration set (12 gestures total)
  • You will have 1 minute to learn and experiment with the first set of 6 gestures and emoticons
  • Then, you will be tested on a sequence of 12 random vibrations selected from the first set of vibrations
  • For each vibration, respond with the corresponding gesture to the best of your abilities
  • After you complete each set, show the alert box to a test administrator
  • You will then be directed to learn and test the second set of gestures
