Welcome to Eyes-Free Interactions Lab!

Thank you for participating in our Human Computer Interaction research project! You will be participating two 2 sets of experiments (total 4 sets).

Here are the instructions for the two experiemnts:

  • Vibration-Emote Experiment
    1. First, you will start with learning 6 different reaction emotes represented by different vibrations. Click each reaction button to learn how each reaction is represented by the vibration.
    2. Keep "learning" reactions until you are comfortable and are confident you can identify which vibration corresponds to which reaction.
    3. Once you are comfortable, you may click proceed button to proceed into the experiment.
    4. In the experiment, you will be given a vibration, 6 reaction buttons and a repeat vibration button. Click the reaction which you think corresponds to the given vibration. You may repeat the vibration by pressing repeat vibration button.
    5. Once you are done with the experiment, share the result page with the Project BAKI team member.
