Thank you for participating in our Human Computer Interaction research project! This experiment is divided into 2 sections: Vibrations and Gestures

Instructions for Part 1: Vibrations

  • In this first section, you will learn 2 different sets of vibrations and their corresponding emoticons
  • There are 6 vibrations in each vibration set (12 vibrations total)
  • You will have 1 minute to learn and experiment with the first set of 6 vibrations and emoticons
  • Then, you will be tested on a sequence of 12 random vibrations selected from the first set of vibrations
  • For each vibration, respond with the corresponding emoticon button to the best of your abilities
  • After you complete each set, show the alert box to a test administrator
  • You will then be directed to learn and test the second set of vibrations
  • Once you complete both vibration sets, you will move onto Part 2: Gestures

Once you have finished both experiments, press the button below to move on to the next experiment.
